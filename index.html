    About Us

    We are a group of young researchers and students from top-ranked universities in Tehran with a passion for creativity which is a basis for a brighter future. Our team is working on soccer match analysis using Machine learning techniques.

    Our Mission

    Given the fact that soccer is the most popular sport in the world, our ultimate goal is to develop optimized software that automatically analyzes the soccer matches and extracts the highlights and playersâ€™ statistics which would be of interest of soccer technical analyzers.

    Our Plan

    We believe that Machine learning will pave the way to analyze the sport automatically and our plan is to create or utilize different data mining tools that make this possible.

    Our Vision

    We treat data as a pivotal asset to our team and combine the knowledge and collaborative explosion of creativity to extract significant information which is the basis for a brighter future.

    Services

    Our services contain a broad range of soccer data analysis from providing multimodal datasets (image, video and sounds) to advanced components such as event detection, virtual advertising and keyword spotting.

    Virtual Advertising

    Using this service, you can advertise your product or service on the stadium billboards virtually and completely by a single software. Another service you can use is overlaying flags and logos of teams or any other picture you want on the grasses.

    Event Detection (Video based)

    Soccer event detection is a foundation of Soccer match analysis. In this section, we detected and classified some of the most important events using soccer video clips which can be of interest of soccer technical analyzer.

    Perspective Transformation

    Using this service, you can transform perspective of football video frame into top view which helps coaches and viewers to extract some information (like speed of players or average Kilo Meters each player runs) about performance of players.

    Event Detection (Image based)

    Using this service, you can process a full video of a soccer match, obtaining all of the events that occurred in the match. The input could be a soccer match with any time duration, while the output, after processing and distinguishing events, is a tagged video.

    Keyword Spotting

    The primary purpose of developing a Keyword Spotting (KS) system is to detect important football events using the voice of a football reporter. This module helped Imaged and video-based event detection to achieve proper results
